Quick Answers

How do I link my YouTube account?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Link YouTube Account". You'll be guided through a secure OAuth authentication process.

Why isn't YouTube Live working?

YouTube Live must be enabled on your channel. New channels may need to wait 24 hours for YouTube to activate live streaming.

What are the minimum requirements?

You need iOS 17.0 or later, a YouTube account with Live enabled, and a stable internet connection.

How do I improve stream quality?

Use a strong Wi-Fi or cellular connection, select appropriate video quality in settings (720p or 1080p), and ensure good lighting.
