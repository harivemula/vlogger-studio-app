Why Is Vlogger Free?

We believe that everyone should have access to professional live streaming tools. Our mission is to democratize content creation and make YouTube live streaming accessible to all creators, regardless of budget. Vlogger is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vlogger really free?

Yes! Vlogger is 100% free to download and use. All features are included with no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or hidden costs.

Are there any limitations on the free version?

No. All features are available to all users. There are no time limits, no watermarks, and no restricted features.

Will there be a paid version in the future?

We're committed to keeping Vlogger free for all users. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Do I need a YouTube account?

Yes, you need a YouTube account with YouTube Live enabled to use Vlogger. The app integrates directly with YouTube's live streaming service.

What are the system requirements?

You need an iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later, a YouTube account, and an active internet connection.

Is there a trial period?

Since Vlogger is completely free, there's no need for a trial period. Simply download the app and start streaming!
