Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

At Vlogger, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Authentication credentials (securely stored)

1.2 YouTube Account Data

When you link your YouTube account, we access:

  • YouTube channel information (name, ID, thumbnail)
  • Live streaming status and permissions
  • OAuth tokens (securely encrypted)
  • Broadcast and stream metadata

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Network connection type

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Enable YouTube live streaming functionality
  • Authenticate and secure your account
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Communicate important updates or changes
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore (user profiles and settings)
  • Firebase Authentication (account credentials)
  • Secure device storage (OAuth tokens via SecureStore)

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for authentication
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited access to user data

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 YouTube/Google

We share limited data with YouTube/Google to enable live streaming features. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube's Terms of Service.

4.2 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, cloud functions
  • Expo - App development and deployment platform

4.3 No Selling of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Revoke YouTube account linking at any time
  • Opt out of certain data collection
  • Export your data (where applicable)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As necessary to provide our services
  • As required by law or legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Vlogger is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Camera and Microphone Access

Vlogger requires access to your device's camera and microphone for live streaming. This access is:

  • Only used during active streaming sessions
  • Not recorded or stored by our app
  • Controlled by device permissions you grant
  • Transmitted directly to YouTube's servers

9. Analytics and Tracking

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our app. This helps us improve functionality and user experience. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification (for material changes)

12. Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and request deletion of your data.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the app's Contact Us page.

14. Consent

By using Vlogger, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
